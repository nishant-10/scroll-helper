# Privacy Policy for Scroll Helper

**Last Updated:** July 2026

## Overview

Scroll Helper is a lightweight Chrome extension that allows users to instantly scroll to the top or bottom of webpages, including modern web applications with custom scroll containers.

Your privacy is important. Scroll Helper is designed to work entirely within your browser and does not collect or transmit your data.

## Data Collection

Scroll Helper **does not collect, store, transmit, or sell** any personal information, browsing history, webpage content, or user activity.

The extension does not require user accounts or sign-in.

## Permissions

Scroll Helper requests access to webpages (`<all_urls>`) solely to:

- Display floating scroll buttons.
- Detect the active scroll container.
- Scroll the current webpage when a user clicks a button.

All processing is performed locally in your browser.

## Remote Code

Scroll Helper does **not** download or execute remote code.

All JavaScript, CSS, and assets are packaged within the extension.

## Third-Party Services

Scroll Helper does not use:

- Analytics services
- Advertising services
- Tracking technologies
- Cookies
- External APIs

## Data Sharing

No user data is collected or shared with any third party.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the extension's functionality changes in future releases.
